Display a Lyft button on your web page to get visitors to take Lyft rides right to your door!

== Description ==

Bring more customers to your business or venue's door with a Ride Here With Lyft button on your page.

Customers that click the button will be linked into the Lyft app with your business destination pre-filled (or prompted to download the app).

== Installation ==

Installation instructions:

1. Upload the `ride-here-with-lyft` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Visit `Appearance` -> `Widgets` to drag the `Ride Here with Lyft` widget into a menu that supports Widgets.

For more details, please visit the [Lyft Web Widgets](https://www.lyft.com/widgets) site.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= I don't see a way to drag the Ride Here With Lyft widget into a menu! =

Make sure that you've configured a Sidebar for use with your theme. Read more on [Sidebars](https://developer.wordpress.org/themes/functionality/sidebars/) from the Wordpress Theme docs.

= I've dragged the Ride Here With Lyft widget onto a menu, but it's not displaying on my site! =

You may be using a theme that either does not make use of Sidebars and Wordpress Widgets, or the Sidebar that you dragged your widget into is not currently active. Please consult your theme documentation for solutions.
